Rhetorical Situation

This assignment asked us to think of a presentation we had that day and describe the rhetorical situation. I used a presentation from class that day in which we had a guest speaker talking about the program she wanted us to develop for her. I assessed how and why she was giving the presentation as well as the purpose.

For my CISC275 class, we have to make a program for a client and that client just gave a presentation on what she expects us to make and her ideas so far. This presentation was given over zoom in the early evening so the dynamic of the presentation was relaxed but professional with her main interaction besides the PowerPoint being her asking if we had any questions. She was presenting to a class of mostly computer science majors so the terms and ideas she was presenting were field-specific but also had to be general enough for the non-CS majors to understand. The presentation’s main purpose was to inform us on how the client would like her program designed and what functionality it would have while also having some fun with the idea of her program.
