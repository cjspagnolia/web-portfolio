In this assignment we were asked to read through an annual report and write a memo assessing the report. I chose to base my report on Microsoft’s 2020 annual report and evaluated the first three sections.

Subject: Less distractions in our meetings.

Good Afternoon Dave,

I hope you are doing well in these trying times. I have a proposition about my schedule and what hours I work which I think will raise the quality of my work. I have a lot of family that I have to take care of in the early parts of the day which takes a toll on my work, especially around the current starting time of 9am. If I was able to start my shift 2 hours later, at 11am and ending at 7pm, I think I would be a more productive employee.

I would greatly appreciate being able to start my workday at 11am at the earliest convenience so I can dedicate more of my undivided attention to work.

I appreciate your time,
CJ Spagnolia
